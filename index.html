Why Groundwork

Change is made of small, repeatable things.

Most environmental progress doesn't come from one big gesture. It comes from thousands of people choosing the slightly harder, slightly better option — again and again, in the same neighborhood, the same week. Groundwork exists to make those choices visible, so the people doing them know they're part of something larger, and the people watching know what to try next.
